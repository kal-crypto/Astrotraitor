Privacy & Cookies Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

AstroTraitors (“the game”, “we”, “us”) is a free browser-based game. This policy explains what information the game handles, how cookies and advertising work, and the choices you have. By playing the game you agree to this policy.

Information the game handles

The game does not require an account and does not ask for your real identity. The only information you provide is what you type to play:

  • A display name and a colour you choose for your character.
  • Gameplay input — your movements and actions, plus any text or voice chat during meetings — which is relayed to the other players in your game so you can play together.

This gameplay and chat data is passed between players for the duration of a match through our relay server and is not stored after the game ends. Voice chat is sent between players and is not recorded. We do not sell your information.

Cookies and advertising

The game is supported by advertising through Google AdSense. To show ads, Google and its partners use cookies and similar technologies:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • These cookies may be used for ad personalisation and measurement.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out of some third-party vendors’ use of cookies for personalised advertising at aboutads.info/choices or, in Europe, youronlinechoices.eu.

For more on how Google uses data, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.

Consent (EEA, UK & Switzerland)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you will be shown a consent message before personalised ads are served, and you can choose whether to consent to the use of cookies for advertising. You can change your choice at any time. This message is provided through a Google-certified consent management platform.

Third-party services

The game relies on these third parties, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdSense — advertising — Google Privacy Policy.
  • Netlify — hosts the game page and may log basic request data such as IP address for security and reliability.
  • Render — hosts the multiplayer relay server that connects players during a match.

Children

This game is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.
